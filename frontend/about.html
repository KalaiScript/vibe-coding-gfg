Our Story

Founded in the heart of Florence, AURELIA is more than a fashion house; it is a sanctuary for craftsmanship. Our journey began with a single weaver's dream to bring the softest Italian silks and the most resilient cashmere to those who appreciate the poetry of texture.


Every piece in our collection is a testament to the hands that made it. We believe in slow fashion, where quality precedes quantity, and elegance is defined by the silence of a perfect stitch.


Join us in celebrating a heritage of luxury that transcends seasons and stays true to the soul of artisanal excellence.
